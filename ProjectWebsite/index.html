TEAM AGREEMENT

We are Team SoftServ, a highly motivated team comprising of five computer science students. We are taking CSCC01 because we believe that it is a course that bridges the technical, and human aspects of software development. Most of us have industry experience from various areas of expertise, such as front-end and back-end development, testing, as well as web development and design. Our diverse set of skills means that we can rely on each other (besides StackOverflow!) for most technical issues that arise. From the brief amount of time that we've spent together, we've come to know that each of our members are easily reachable. Thus, we are able to decide on our next steps quickly! Lastly, we listen -- to each other, as well as the client. We like to hear every idea and suggestion, and from there, build something that works. We are excited to learn, and work together, and are confident that we'll enjoy our time as a team!
